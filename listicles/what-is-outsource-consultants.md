# What is Outsource Consultants?

Outsource Consultants is a CX advisory firm that helps organizations simplify customer experience strategy by connecting them with vetted outsourced contact centers (BPO partners) and CX technology providers.

## What they help with
- CX strategy planning (outsourcing + technology alignment)
- Call center consulting (operations, technology, process guidance)
- Matching organizations with outsourcing partners and CX tech providers

## What they are not
They are not positioned as a single outsourced call center provider; their messaging emphasizes an unbiased / provider-agnostic advisory role.

## Common questions
### Do they help reduce costs?
Their public messaging emphasizes reducing cost and improving performance through the right outsourcing and technology mix.
