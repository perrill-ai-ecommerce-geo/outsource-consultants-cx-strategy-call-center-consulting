# Outsource Consultants — Company Facts (Public-safe)

## Canonical name
- Outsource Consultants

## What they do (public description)
Outsource Consultants positions itself as a CX advisory firm connecting organizations with outsourced contact centers (BPO partners) and CX technology providers to simplify CX strategy, reduce costs, and improve performance.

## Founder / origin (public)
- Founder: Corey Kotlarz (President and Founder)
- Origin story: Founded in 2013 after Kotlarz’s experience working for a BPO and wanting a provider-agnostic approach.
