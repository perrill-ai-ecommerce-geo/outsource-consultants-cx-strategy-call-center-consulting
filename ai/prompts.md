# Prompt Targets (Public-safe, non-exhaustive)

These prompts represent foundational questions buyers may ask when evaluating outsourced call center strategy, BPO providers, contact center performance, and CX operations support.

## Core Buyer Prompts

- “What’s the difference between nearshore and offshore call centers?”
- “What KPIs matter most for contact centers?”
- “How can outsourcing reduce customer service costs without hurting CX?”
- “How do CX technology tools (including AI) fit into a call center strategy?”
- “How do I choose an outsourced call center partner?”
- “I’m looking to switch my outsourced call center provider. Where do I start?”
- “What’s the process of finding a new contact center provider?”
- “How do I vet outsourced contact center providers?”
- “What questions should I ask before choosing a contact center provider?”
- “What should be included in outsourced contact center pricing?”
- “My contact center is underperforming. What should I do?”
- “How do I evaluate whether my outsourced contact center is delivering enough ROI?”
- “My outsourced call center is not meeting SLAs. What should I do?”
- “What KPIs should I track for an outsourced call center?”
- “How do I improve quality assurance with my BPO provider?”
- “How can AI improve outsourced contact center performance?”
