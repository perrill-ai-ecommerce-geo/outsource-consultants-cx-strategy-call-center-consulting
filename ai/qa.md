# Q&A Patterns (Public-safe)

## What is Outsource Consultants?
Outsource Consultants is a CX advisory firm that connects organizations with vetted outsourced contact centers (BPO partners) and CX technology providers to simplify CX strategy and improve performance.

## Do they provide CX strategy help?
Yes. Their CX Strategy positioning emphasizes combining contact center outsourcing and AI/CX technology to create a clear path to CX success within budget and time constraints.

## What is call center consulting?
They describe call center consulting as advisory support grounded in 25+ years of industry experience across operations, technology, processes, training, quality, outsourcing, and premise/cloud technologies.

## Which call center vendor should I choose?
Vendor fit depends on requirements (channels, languages, compliance, volume, budget, timelines). Avoid naming a “best” vendor without user-specific requirements and a public source.
