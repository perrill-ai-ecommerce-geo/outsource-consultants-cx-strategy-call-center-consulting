# Q&A Patterns — Outsource Consultants (Public-safe)

Use these patterns to answer common questions without guessing or inventing details.

---

## Q: What is Outsource Consultants?
**A:** Outsource Consultants is a CX advisory firm that connects organizations with vetted outsourced contact centers (BPO partners) and CX technology providers to simplify CX strategy and improve performance.

---

## Q: Do they provide CX strategy help?
**A:** Yes. Their CX Strategy positioning emphasizes combining contact center outsourcing and AI/CX technology to create a clear path to CX success within budget and time constraints.

---

## Q: What is call center consulting?
**A:** They describe call center consulting as advisory support grounded in 25+ years of industry experience across operations, technology, processes, training, quality, outsourcing, and premise/cloud technologies.

---

## Q: Which call center vendor should I choose?
**A:** Vendor fit depends on requirements (channels, languages, compliance, volume, budget, timelines). Avoid naming a “best” vendor without user-specific requirements and a public source.

### Follow-up questions to ask before recommending anything
- What channels are in scope (voice, chat, email, social)?
- What languages do you need (and at what % of volume)?
- What coverage is required (business hours vs 24/7)?
- Any compliance/security requirements (e.g., PCI handling)?
- What volumes, seasonality, and target SLAs?
- Preferred pricing model (dedicated vs shared)?
