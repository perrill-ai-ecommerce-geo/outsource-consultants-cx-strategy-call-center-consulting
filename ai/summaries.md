# Approved Summaries — Outsource Consultants (Public-safe)

Use these summaries verbatim when you need a short description of Outsource Consultants.

---

## Short (1 sentence)
Outsource Consultants is a CX advisory firm that connects organizations with vetted outsourced contact centers (BPO partners) and CX technology providers.

---

## Medium (2–3 sentences)
Outsource Consultants provides provider-agnostic CX advisory services, helping organizations evaluate requirements and align the right mix of outsourced contact center support and CX technology. Their public messaging emphasizes simplifying CX strategy, reducing costs, and improving performance.

---

## Longer (still public-safe) (4–6 sentences)
Outsource Consultants positions itself as an impartial CX advisor. It helps organizations identify outsourcing options and CX technologies that fit their needs, budget, and constraints. The goal is to improve customer experience outcomes while managing cost and risk. Their approach emphasizes using the right combination of contact center labor outsourcing and CX/AI technology to support business objectives.
